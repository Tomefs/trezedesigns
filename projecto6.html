"Lamb to the Slaughter" the board game


Project created as part of the Multimedia Applications course in the Design and Multimedia Degree Program at the University of Coimbra.


Project carried out in the third year of university in the first semester in a group of four people.
For this project, we had to choose a literary work from a set that was given to us and use it to create a multimedia project such as a book, a comic strip, or a game. We chose Roald Dahl's “Lamb to the Slaughter” and decided to create a board game inspired by the story, similar to games such as Cluedo or Escape Rooms. This whole process required us to create a set of rules for the game, a cohesive visual identity inspired by the aesthetics of the era in which the story was written (1953), and a trailer to present it.
