"Scribble Learning" an educational app


Project created as part of the Interactive Media Design course in the Design and Multimedia Degree Program at the University of Coimbra.


Our proposal consisted of designing an application that supports children with special educational needs in their learning, communication, and integration. Our application has the flexibility to be used in a classroom setting, through individual and group educational games, which aim to encourage communication and relationships among all children participating in the activity. It can also be used at home, under the supervision of parents or any other legal guardian, so that they can continuously monitor their learning.


Our goal was to create an application with exercises and activities for the educational needs of pre-primary and primary school children in a learning context.


1. Learning colors; 2. Learning numbers; 3. Learning letters; 4. Motor coordination; 5. Communication; Etc.


All the application was designed with Figma.
